Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrumptious scrambled eggs are soft, creamy, and velvety, with a delicate texture that melts in your mouth. They're gently cooked to perfection, with just the right balance of fluffiness and richness.

The golden-yellow color hints at the buttery flavor that coats each bite, and the eggs are lightly seasoned with salt and pepper, allowing the natural taste to shine. If you like, a bit of cheese or fresh herbs can be folded in, adding an extra layer of indulgence. Each forkful is comforting, warm, and satisfyingâ€”like a cozy morning on a plate.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 teaspoons butter

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs with salt until completely combined and very foamy.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over low heat until bubbling. Add whisked eggs; cook, stirring frequently for even, moist curds, until thickened and creamy, but still shiny, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Remove scrambled eggs from the pan before they begin to look dry, as they will continue to thicken off the heat.
